## ðŸš« What NOT To Do (Spam Triggers)

### Subject Line Killers
- ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation!!!
- "Quick question" (overdone)
- "Re:" or "Fwd:" when it's not a reply
- Dollar signs or "free" / "discount"
- Urgency words: "Act now", "Limited time"

### Body Copy Mistakes
- "I hope this email finds you well"
- "Just following up" (as opener)
- Walls of text (keep under 150 words)
- Multiple CTAs (pick ONE)
- Attachments in first email
- Generic company descriptions
- Talking about yourself before their problem

### Technical Spam Triggers
- Too many links (max 1-2)
- Images in cold emails
- Tracked links with suspicious domains
- No unsubscribe option (if bulk sending)
