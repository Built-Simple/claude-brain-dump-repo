## 5. Pricing Models to Consider

### For API/Integration Partners (POS, CRM)

| Model | Structure | When to Use |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Per-Response** | $0.05-0.15 per AI-generated response | Usage-based, low commitment |
| **Per-Location/Month** | $5-15/location/month | Predictable revenue |
| **Revenue Share** | 20-40% of what partner charges | For larger partners |
| **Bundled License** | Flat annual fee for unlimited | Enterprise deals |

**Recommended:** Start with **per-location pricing ($8-12/mo)** for integration partners. Matches RightResponse AI pricing while allowing margin for the partner.

### For White-Label/Agency Partners

| Model | Structure | When to Use |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Tiered SaaS** | $99-499/mo based on locations | Agency subscriptions |
| **Per-Client Pricing** | $15-25/client/month | Agencies with few clients |
| **Revenue Share** | 30-40% to ReviewMaster | High-volume partners |
| **One-Time License + Support** | $25-50k + 15-25% annual | OEM/Enterprise white-label |

**Recommended:** Offer **tiered agency pricing** with white-label at $200+/month for 10+ locations, scaling down per-location as volume increases.

### Reference: Competitor Pricing

- **RightResponse AI:** $8/location/mo (annual) or $10/location/mo (monthly)
- **GatherUp:** $99/mo (1 location) → $25/mo (101+ locations)
